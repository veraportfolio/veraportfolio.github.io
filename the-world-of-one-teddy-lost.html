The World of One Teddy Lost

Introducing Puddle Jump Studios exciting debut game!

Set in a world ruined by nuclear and climate apocalypse, navigate an abandoned and dilapidated factory in search of your stolen Teddy Bear, your best friend and only companion. One Teddy Lost is a stealthy, puzzle game with elements of “kid-friendly” horror that is inspired by works such as BioShock, Fallout, Coraline and Little Nightmares.

The flooded factory you must explore belonged to none other than the company “Just Add Water! Inc.” This fictitious company revolutionized the hydration technology which allowed objects to be contained in small capsules and then hydrated (thus “just add water!”) to create a full-sized, functional version of said object. Player’s will have to utilize this bizarre technology to solve puzzles and escape the factory.

Puzzles are not the only thing standing in the player’s way, however. Something has made this abandoned factory its home and is not too keen on uninvited guests...
